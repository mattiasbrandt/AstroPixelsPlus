Hardware Gadgets

Feature toggles for optional hardware modules. Preferences are saved to NVS and applied after reboot.
BadMotivator (Smoke)
Controls smoke output through the BadMotivator module.
Key: badmot | Commands: #BMSMOKE, #BMSTOP, #BMPULSE<sec>
FireStrip (Fire LEDs)
Controls the optional auxiliary fire/spark LED strip.
Key: firest | Commands: #FIRESPARK<ms>, #FIREBURN<ms>, #FIRESTOP
Charge Bay Indicator (CBI)
Enables MAX7219 charge-bay panel animation output.
Key: cbienb | Typical commands: #CBION, #CBIOFF, #CBISET<n>
DataPanel
Enables MAX7219 body datapanel animation output.
Key: dpenab | Typical commands: #DPON, #DPOFF, #DPSET<n>
Hardware must be physically connected and compiled in with the corresponding build flags.
